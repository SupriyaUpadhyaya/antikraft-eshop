Help

Placing an Order

To place an order, follow these simple steps:

  1. Browse our website and add the desired items to your shopping cart.
  2. Proceed to the checkout page.
  3. Provide your shipping and billing information.
  4. Select your preferred payment method and complete the payment.
  5. Review your order summary and confirm your purchase.

Tracking an Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your shipment on our website or the shipping carrier's website.

Returns and Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Please visit our Returns and Refunds page for detailed instructions on how to initiate a return and our refund policy.
